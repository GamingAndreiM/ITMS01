Fibonacci Sequence

In mathematics, the Fibonacci sequence is a sequence in which each number is the sum of the two preceding ones.

Lucas Sequence

The Lucas sequence is an integer sequence named after the mathematician François Édouard Anatole Lucas, who studied both that sequence and the closely related Fibonacci sequence.

Tribonacci Sequence

Tribonacci numbers are like the Fibonacci numbers, but instead of starting with two predetermined terms, the sequence starts with three predetermined terms and each term afterwards is the sum of the preceding three terms.
